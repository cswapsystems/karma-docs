# phase 2 roadmap

BTC Karma begins with Bitcoin staking and KARMA rewards, but the long-term vision is much larger.

Phase 2 is designed to make BTC Karma a gateway for Bitcoin holders to access exclusive DeFi opportunities. With the ultimate goal being to return yield to Bitcoin holders in $BTC.

## Phase 1

Phase 1 focuses on:

* Bitcoin staking
* KARMA rewards
* Enlightenment Levels
* Leaderboard progression
* Bitcoin-first onboarding
* Reward destination verification

## Phase 2

Phase 2 is designed to expand BTC Karma into a broader Bitcoin-native DeFi gateway.

Potential Phase 2 features may include:

* Bitcoin-backed stablecoin borrowing
* Cardano stablecoin deployment
* Composable DeFi strategies
* RWA liquidity opportunities
* RWA purchases with passive yield
* Lending and borrowing credit spread opportunities
* One-click yield strategies
* Potential returns denominated in Bitcoin or stable assets

## Limited Capacity Opportunities

Some future opportunities (high APR) will have limited capacity.

When demand exceeds supply, BTC Karma may use Enlightenment Levels and leaderboard status to determine priority access.

$KARMA utility is access to higher yield for Bitcoin holders in both Phase 1 and Phase 2.

## Bitcoin-First Experience

The long-term goal is for Bitcoin holders to access Cardano-powered opportunities without needing to feel like deFi power users.

BTC Karma may use Cardano infrastructure underneath, but the product is simple, accessible, and Bitcoin-first.
