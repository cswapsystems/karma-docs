BTC Karma begins with BTC staking rewards and expands toward future Bitcoin yield access.

Phase 1 is designed to help users stake BTC, earn KARMA, optionally buy KARMA through the platform, and increase their Enlightenment Level.

Phase 2 is designed to expand BTC Karma beyond token rewards and into Bitcoin-powered yield access.

## Phase 1

Phase 1 is designed around a simple user journey:

1. Stake BTC.
2. Earn KARMA rewards.
3. Optionally buy KARMA through the BTC Karma staking platform.
4. Raise your Enlightenment Level.
5. Unlock stronger reward potential.
6. Prepare for future Bitcoin yield opportunities.

Users may earn KARMA based on their Bitcoin staking activity, active emissions, and Enlightenment Level. Buying KARMA is another optional path to raise Enlightenment Level faster.

As users earn, buy, and hold KARMA, they may progress into higher Enlightenment Levels.

Higher levels are designed to increase reward potential during Phase 1 and may improve access to future BTC Karma opportunities.

## Phase 2

Phase 2 is designed to expand BTC Karma beyond KARMA rewards and into Bitcoin-native yield access.

Potential Phase 2 features may include:

* Bitcoin-backed borrowing
* Stablecoin deployment
* Curated yield strategies
* RWA yield opportunities
* Lending and borrowing opportunities
* Bitcoin-first user experiences that keep the workflow simple
* Potential returns denominated in Bitcoin or stable assets, subject to protocol rules and market conditions

## Limited Capacity Opportunities

Some future Bitcoin yield opportunities may have limited capacity.

If demand exceeds available allocation, higher Enlightenment Levels may receive priority access.

KARMA supports Enlightenment Level progression, reward potential, harvest capacity, and access to future Bitcoin yield opportunities.

## Bitcoin-First Experience

The long-term goal is for Bitcoin holders to access future Bitcoin yield opportunities through a simple Bitcoin-first workflow.

BTC Karma may use Cardano infrastructure for reward accounting, token distribution, and future yield access, but the product is simple, accessible, and Bitcoin-first.
