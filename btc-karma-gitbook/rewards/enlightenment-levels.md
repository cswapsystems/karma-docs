Enlightenment Levels are BTC Karma's level system.

They are designed to make Phase 1 participation matter beyond the first reward period.

As users stake BTC, earn or buy KARMA, and hold KARMA over time, they may progress through higher levels.

Higher levels can matter in two ways:

* During Phase 1, they may increase KARMA reward potential.
* During active reward periods, they may affect how much a user can harvest.
* In future phases, they may improve access to limited-capacity Bitcoin yield opportunities.

## Raising Your Enlightenment Level

Users can raise their Enlightenment Level by accumulating and holding KARMA.

There are two primary ways to acquire KARMA:

1. Earn KARMA through Bitcoin staking rewards.
2. Buy KARMA directly through the BTC Karma staking platform.

Buying KARMA is optional. It gives users another path to raise their Enlightenment Level faster, while staking rewards remain a core way to accumulate KARMA over time.

On the Rewards page, users can click **Raise Level of Enlightenment** to see how much KARMA is needed to reach each higher level.

This gives users a clear path to increase their level over time.

Higher Enlightenment Levels are designed to improve reward potential during Phase 1 and may provide priority access to limited-capacity Bitcoin yield opportunities in future phases.

Want to reach a higher level faster? Visit the Rewards page and click **Raise Level of Enlightenment** to see how much KARMA you need for each higher tier.

## Why Enlightenment Levels Matter

Higher Enlightenment Levels may unlock:

* Enhanced reward multipliers
* Increased harvest capacity during active reward periods
* Improved leaderboard status
* Priority access to future yield opportunities
* Improved access to limited-capacity Phase 2 opportunities
* Additional access benefits as BTC Karma evolves

## Enlightenment Levels and Harvesting

Enlightenment Levels may affect how much a user can harvest from available rewards.

Higher levels are designed to increase a user's reward potential and may increase harvest capacity during active reward periods. Actual harvest amounts depend on staking activity, active emissions, protocol rules, reward availability, and the user's Enlightenment Level.

This makes Enlightenment Level progression important during Phase 1 and may improve access to future Bitcoin opportunities.

## Level Structure

BTC Karma has 5 Enlightenment Levels.

The exact thresholds may be updated as the platform grows, but the system is designed to reward users who keep participating over time.

## Proposed Launch Levels

| Level   | Name         | Description                                             |
| ------- | ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------- |
| Level 1 | Grounded     | Entry-level participant beginning the BTC Karma journey |
| Level 2 | Meditative   | Active participant with a growing KARMA position        |
| Level 3 | Illuminated  | Meaningful BTC Karma participant                        |
| Level 4 | Transcendent | Strong long-term participant                            |
| Level 5 | Enlightened  | Highest-level participants with priority status         |

## How Levels Are Determined

Enlightenment Levels may consider:

* KARMA balance
* Staking activity
* KARMA acquired through the BTC Karma staking platform
* Length of participation
* Reward history
* Leaderboard position
* BTC Karma participation

The goal is to make levels difficult to fake and meaningful to maintain.

## Why This Matters for Phase 2

In Phase 2, some Bitcoin yield opportunities may have limited capacity.

For example, a curated RWA or DeFi opportunity may only support a limited allocation. If demand exceeds available allocation, higher Enlightenment Levels may receive priority access.

KARMA has a role beyond emissions: users can earn, buy, and hold KARMA to raise their Enlightenment Level and improve access to future Bitcoin opportunities.
