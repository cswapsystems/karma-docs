# enlightenment levels

Enlightenment Levels are BTC Karma's progression system.

They are designed to reward long-term ecosystem alignment, not just short-term staking activity.

As users participate in BTC Karma, accumulate KARMA, and remain active over time, they may progress through higher levels.

## Why Enlightenment Levels Matter

Higher Enlightenment Levels may unlock:

* Increased reward potential
* Improved leaderboard status
* Priority access to future yield opportunities
* Exclusive Phase 2 opportunities
* Additional platform benefits as BTC Karma evolves

## Level Structure

BTC Karma has 5 Enlightenment Levels.

The exact thresholds may be updated as the platform grows, but the system is designed to reward users who remain aligned with BTC Karma over time.

## Proposed Launch Levels

| Level   | Name         | Description                                             |
| ------- | ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------- |
| Level 1 | Grounded     | Entry-level participant beginning the BTC Karma journey |
| Level 2 | Meditative   | Active participant with growing alignment               |
| Level 3 | Illuminated  | Meaningful ecosystem participant                        |
| Level 4 | Transcendent | Strong long-term participant                            |
| Level 5 | Enlightened  | Highest-alignment participants with priority status     |

## How Levels Are Determined

Enlightenment Levels may consider:

* KARMA balance
* Staking activity
* Length of participation
* Reward history
* Leaderboard position
* Ecosystem alignment

The goal is to make levels difficult to fake and meaningful to maintain.

## Why This Matters for Phase 2

In Phase 2, some yield opportunities may have limited capacity.

For example, a high-quality RWA or DeFi opportunity may only support a limited amount of capital. If demand exceeds available capacity, higher Enlightenment Levels may receive priority access.

This gives $KARMA utility beyond simple emissions. Ultimately $KARMA provides access to high yield opportunities for Bitcoin throughout the BTC Karma long term roadmap.
